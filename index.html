About Us

Royal Touch is a beauty salon that has been proudly catering to the needs of our clients for over 30 years.We offer premium quality beauty treatments like hairdressing, makeup, and skincare. All of our services are provided by accredited and fully-trained, professional and friendly staff in a comfortable environment.If you are attracted by the idea of excellent customer service with top of the line hair and makeup products, we hope to hear from you soon!
